Real Risks We Find

What's hiding in your AI stack

HIGH
Cross-Border Transfer Without Safeguards
Every time staff paste customer data into ChatGPT or Gemini, it leaves Kenya — violating Section 48 with zero contractual protection.
HIGH
No AI-Specific Data Subject Consent
Section 30 requires explicit, AI-specific consent. Your existing policy almost certainly doesn't cover AI tools — making every AI-processed interaction unlawful.
MEDIUM
No Internal Staff AI Policy
Without a policy, staff share National IDs and financial records with consumer AI tools daily. One incident creates breach notification obligations under Section 43.
MEDIUM
Unregistered Data Controller
If you process personal data (which you do, with any AI tool), you must register with the ODPC. Most SMEs have never done this.
